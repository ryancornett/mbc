GOSPEL-DRIVEN CHURCH

We are a community of believers whose lives have been changed by God, and He now gathers us together as part of Jesus Christ's Church to 1) worship Him in spirit and in truth, 2) teach His ways, and 3) contribute to the spread of the good news of the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ all over the world. We are commanded to love God and love our neighbors. We are committed to obey and serve God and others.
