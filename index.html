Manchester Baptist Church

we are a

GOSPEL-DRIVEN CHURCH

We are a community of believers whose lives have been changed by God, and He now gathers us together as part of Jesus Christ's Church to:

  • worship Him in spirit and truth
  • teach His ways
  • help spread of the Gospel of our Lord Jesus all over the world

We are commanded to love God and love our neighbors. We are committed to obey and serve God and others.
